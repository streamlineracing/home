Our Purpose

We are Streamline Racing, the newest F1 team at Wheeler High School in Georgia. Our goal is to succeed at competitions while learning and reaching new depths of understanding. We hope to be able to push the boundaries of how fast our car can race and how innovative our minds can become. The purpose of this team is to stick together to help each other overcome obstacles and persevere until we reach the finish line. Explore the website to learn more about us!
